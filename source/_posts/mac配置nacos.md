### 下载

https://github.com/alibaba/nacos/releases/tag/2.3.1

### 解压

```shell
unzip nacos-server-1.4.3.zip
```

### 运行

```shell
cd /usr/local/nacos/bin 
sudo su
sh startup.sh -m standalone
```
